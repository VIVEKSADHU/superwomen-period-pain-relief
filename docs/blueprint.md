# **App Name**: Period Peace

## Core Features:

- Digital Guide Access: Provide instant access to the 'Menstrual Cramps No More' digital guide (PDF format) upon purchase.
- Emergency Cramp Relief: Offer a printable 1-page PDF with an emergency cramp relief routine for quick access.
- Customized Tea Recipes: Provide a cheat sheet with herbal tea recipes tailored to specific period symptoms, and guidance on when to avoid certain teas.
- Beginner-Friendly Yoga: Offer illustrated yoga poses suitable for beginners, with a focus on gentle movements that can be done even in bed.
- Gentle Yoga Calendar: Provide a printable 7-day yoga calendar that is AI powered by prompting for 'gentle yoga', making suggestions with considerations on pose difficulty.
- Pain Trigger Tracking: Enable pain tracking and help women understand and manage their symptoms.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft lavender (#E6E6FA) to promote calmness and serenity.
- Background color: Warm cream (#F5F5DC), desaturated to 20%, for a gentle and inviting feel.
- Accent color: Blush pink (#F08080), with increased brightness and saturation, to highlight key elements.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif, will create a blend of modern readability and approachability
- Use simple, hand-drawn style icons in blush pink and soft lavender.
- Design a mobile-first layout optimized for Instagram ad traffic, with short, scannable sections and clear CTAs.
- Implement subtle animations like fade-ins and smooth scrolling to enhance user experience.
